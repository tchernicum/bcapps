Sending from a location

Visit any MoneyGram agent location.
Fill out a "send" form and give it to a MoneyGram agent.
Share the transaction reference number with your recipient.

Receiving Money

Ask the sender for your reference number.
Visit any MoneyGram location with your reference number and ID.
Complete the "receiver form" and you will be given your money.

Paying at a location

  1. Find your biller using Biller Search.
  2. Find agents in your area using the Agent Locator.
  3. Take your payment information and cash to the agent to complete your transaction and get your receipt.

Paying online

  1. Find your biller using Biller Search.
  2. Create your online profile, entering your payment information.
  3. Submit your payment. You're done!
